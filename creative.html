Creative Work

Photography

As part of my hobbies, I sometimes enjoy photography using my mobile phone. I like capturing moments, scenes, and small details that catch my eye in everyday life. Whether it’s the warm glow of a sunset, the busy streets, or the smile of a friend, photography helps me preserve memories and appreciate the beauty around me. It’s a relaxing and creative way to express how I see the world.

3D Design

I also enjoy working on 3D design to turn my imagination into something visual. It allows me to bring creative ideas to life, whether it’s designing objects, concepts, or spaces that only existed in my thoughts before. 3D design gives me the freedom to experiment with shapes, colors, and textures, helping me visualize and share ideas in a more realistic and engaging way..
